Due Date
--------

Todo.txt supports due dates via the "due" key/value pair. Dates are stored in
the format YYYY-MM-DD.

    (A) Pay taxes due:2018-04-17

When creating a task either enter the exact due date, or use relative dates:

    - yesterday, today, tomorrow
    - monday ... sunday (also supports 3 letter abbreviations e.g. mon)

Related Topics: threshold, due
