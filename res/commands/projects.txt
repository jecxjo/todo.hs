Lists all tasks in groups based on project

    $ todo add "Pay water bills +Home"
    $ todo add "Pay electric bills +Home"
    $ todo add "Add help to +todo"
    $ todo projects
    ==== +Home ====
    1: Pay water bills +Home
    2: Pay electric bills +Home

    ==== +todo ====
    3: Add help to +todo

If tasks have multiple projects, or are part of subprojects (+Project.SubProject)
they show up in multiple groups.
