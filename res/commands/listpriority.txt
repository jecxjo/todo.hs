List priority tasks with optional search criteria

    $ todo list
    1: (A) Do @taxes
    2: (B) Pay cell @bills
    3: (B) Pay electric @bills
    4: Create new budget @bills
    $ todo listpriority @bills
    2: (B) Pay cell @bills
    3: (B) Pay electric @bills

The list command supports optional filters for contexts and projects. List will find matches that
contain all filter options.

Alias: lsp
Related Commands: list, search, priority
